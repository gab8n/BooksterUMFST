About Us

What is Bookster ?

Bookster is the first modern public library in Romania. Since the library opened in 2013, the 77,000 readers have made more than 4 million loans. They have free access to over 100,000 books, articles, case studies, ebooks and videos in various fields, from personal and professional development, to hobbies, fiction and children's books, by accessing www.bookster.ro.
